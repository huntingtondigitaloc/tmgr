Meeting Minutes



TMGR General Membership Meeting
Davey Dogwood Park, Palestine, TX
October 16, 2021

Minutes taken by Wayne Kube, in the absence of TMGR Secretary Moose “Two Deer” Stovall.

President Nick Pappas called the meeting to order at 12:12 pm CDT.

Due to the absence of Tom “I’m busy getting my daughter married” Luin, President Pappas gave a brief treasurer’s report. He reported that TMGR had about $18,000 in the bank. That total did not include all income or expenses related to the Fall GoF. President Pappas stated that there was no reason to consider a membership dues increase.

President Pappas gave an update on the TMGR website changes, plans for the future, and introduced Andrew Allen, whose company has taken over the website. There will be no cost to TMGR for the website support.

Wayne Kube reported on the change of regalia supplier to Jim & Jen Wagner at Jim Wagner Signs. Info is available in Backroads and will be available on the TMGR website.

President Pappas reported on the survey that was taken after the Spring GoF in Killeen, and changes that have been implemented accordingly.

There will be a Christmas Drive on Saturday December 11, 2021 in lieu of a TMGR Christmas party. It will be an overnight event, with a hotel to be named in Hye, TX.

Greg Poffenbarger will host a picnic at his home and new shop on Saturday, November 13.

Discussions included the Rallye at the GoF, the Shopping Scavenger Hunt, and to give your GoF event photos to Russell Read for inclusion at the banquet.

The meeting was adjourned just after 12:30 pm.
