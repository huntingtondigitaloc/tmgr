Our Mission

  • To further the general enjoyment of our hobby and promote awareness of the marque.
  • To promote, organize, and supervise outings, meetings, shows, events, and Gatherings of the Faithful.
  • To help our fellow enthusiasts with technical and non-technical challenges with their vehicles.
  • To build fellowship and new friendships through activities with or without our cars.
  • To foster working relationships with local MG clubs through collaboration and cross-promotion.
  • To promote collaboration with all national and international MG organizations.
