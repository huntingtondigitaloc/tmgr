Spring 2022 GoF Schedule

Thursday, April 28, 2022
2:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. Registration Open: First Floor of Holiday Inn – Henderson, TX
2:45 p.m. RUSSELL & RANDY’S MUSEUM DRIVE DAY
Depart from Holiday Inn in Henderson TX for drive to Kilgore! The Texas Broadcast Museum (416 E. Main St. – Kilgore, TX / $6 admission) has agreed to open JUST FOR THE TMGR! If you don’t want to caravan with Russell and Randy, the museum closes at 5pm FYI.
Caravan Leaders = Russell Read and Randy Hill
Dinner Dinner is on your own, but suggested Henderson locations include (a) Tia Juanita’s Fish Camp at 405 Highway 79 North, and (b) the Jalopeno Tree at 300 Highway 79.

Friday, April 29, 2022
9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Registration Open: First Floor of Holiday Inn – Henderson, TX
1:00 p.m. RUSSELL & NICK’s WILDFLOWER & WINE DRIVE
Depart from Holiday Inn in Henderson TX for an authentic East Texas wildflower and wine drive. Pay your own way at the wineries, and get ready for a 4+ hour drive.
Caravan Leaders = Russell Read and Nick Pappas
Dinner Dinner is on your own, but suggested Henderson locations include (a) Tia Juanita’s Fish Camp at 405 Highway 79 North, and (b) the Jalopeno Tree at 300 Highway 79.

Saturday, April 30, 2022
Oh-dark-thirty Spit and Polish those beautiful MGs! Bucket and water available on the back side of hotel.
7:30 a.m. to 9:00 a.m. Registration Open: First Floor of Holiday Inn – Henderson, TX
9:45 a.m. Depart in Parade with Police Escort to Car Show
Henderson Civic Center – 1500 Lake Forest Parkway.
10:00 a.m. Car Show and judging at the Henderson Civic Center
Noon Lunch Inside Civic Center and TMGR Membership Meeting
1:00 p.m. Drivers depart for “On Your Own Driving Tours” around the beautiful roads of Henderson or the Heavenly Henderson Rallye.
6:15 p.m. Social Hour – Henderson Civic Center – 1500 Lake Forest Parkway
7:00 p.m. Dinner, Awards & Auction at the Henderson Civic Center

Sunday, May 1, 2022
Prepare the car for the drive home and share stories of the weekend. Remember, SAFETY FAST!
About Us

The purpose of our club is to have FUN with MGs, maintain the marque and enrich the MG owner/enthusiast experience through social collaboration with fellow like-minded members, who are united in encouraging the restoration, preservation, and driving of MGs.
